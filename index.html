What is Cub Scouts?

Cub Scouts is the part of Scouting America made just for younger kids. It’s a year of hands-on adventure, real friendships, and growing confidence — with families right alongside their Scouts the whole way.

For kids in grades K–5. Every grade has its own rank to grow into:

  • KindergartenLion
  • 1st gradeTiger
  • 2nd gradeWolf
  • 3rd gradeBear
  • 4th gradeWebelos
  • 5th gradeArrow of Light

Questions from parents

How do we get started?

Easy — come to Join Night on Thursday, August 20, 2026, 6:30–8:00 PM at Messiah Lutheran, 801 S Green St, Brownsburg. Meet the families, ask your questions, and sign up right there. Can’t make it? Email us anytime at pack377bb@gmail.com.

Does my child need any experience?

Not at all. Every Scout starts somewhere, and most kids are brand new when they join. We’ll help you and your child find their footing from day one.

Do we have to go camping?

Camping is one of the best parts of the year, but you go at your family’s pace. There’s plenty of fun close to home — the pinewood derby, service projects, and pack events — alongside the outdoor adventures.

How much time does it take?

Pack 377 plans activities across the whole year so families can pick what fits their schedule. Come to what you can — we’re a family-first pack and we get that life is busy.

Who runs the pack?

Pack 377 is led by volunteer parents and family members, just like you. It runs because families pitch in together — and there’s a place for every grown-up who wants to help. Questions? Reach us at pack377bb@gmail.com.
